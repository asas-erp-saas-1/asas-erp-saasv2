# EXECUTIVE COMMAND CENTER

## 1. Overview
The global viewport for ERP operation. Exposes true state across hyper-scale distributed nodes without crushing the database under metrics aggregation.

## 2. Dashboards
- `GlobalTenantHealthMatrix`: Correlates FinOps spend vs Churn Risk Signals per agency.
- `IncidentIntelligenceCenter`: Provides the root-cause trace timeline. When a regional degradation triggers the DegradedModeRouter, the I.I.C. renders the exact failing sub-component circuit breaker.
- `DistributedQueueObservatory`: Visualizes the 256 logical queue partition velocities across US and EU zones. Uncovers hash-collisions or hot-partitions organically.

## 3. Autonomous Feedback Vis
- The center exposes the logs of the `AutoRemediationEngine`. SREs can review why a specific Kubernetes Worker Pod was evicted autonomously and formally certify the engine's decision model.
